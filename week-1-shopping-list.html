Week 1 Shopping List

This shopping list has been planned so ingredients work across multiple meals and help reduce waste. All quantities are based on a family of 2 adults and 2 children. Feel free to adjust everything to suit your family — nothing is set in stone.

Fresh

  • 5 onions
  • 1 garlic bulb
  • 4 carrots
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lettuce
  • 1 bunch spring onions
  • 1.5kg potatoes
  • 750g baby potatoes

Meat & Fish

  • 8–10 boneless chicken thighs
  • 10 pork sausages
  • 500g beef mince
  • 500–750g pork (steaks or strips)
  • 300g frozen king prawns
  • 2 x 10 packs fish fingers

Dairy

  • 400–500g grated cheddar cheese
  • 300ml double cream or single cream

Bakery

  • 1 pack tortilla wraps
  • 1 pack flatbreads
  • 1 pack burger buns

Frozen

  • 1 bag frozen peas
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables

Dry Goods

  • 500g pasta
  • 500g long grain rice
  • 300g noodles

Store Cupboard

  • Oil, salt & pepper
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Sweet chilli sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Mixed herbs

Shopping List Optimised: Chicken, cheese, potatoes, onions and frozen veg are used across multiple meals to help reduce waste.
