Prawn Fried Rice

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves 4
Mid cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 1Main Ingredients
  • 300 gcooked rice (cold works best)
  • 250 gfrozen raw prawns (defrosted)
  • 2eggs (beaten)
  • 150 gfrozen peas or mixed vegetables
  • 1Store Cupboard / Assumed
  • 1–2 tbspCooking oil
  • 2–3 tbspSoy sauce
  • 1/2 tspGarlic granules
  • 1/2 tspGround ginger
  • 1/4 tspBlack pepper

Method

1

Heat a little oil in a large frying pan or wok over medium heat.

2

Add the prawns and cook for 2–3 minutes until pink and cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside.

3

Add a little more oil to the pan if needed, then pour in the beaten eggs. Scramble lightly until just set.

4

Add the cooked rice, frozen vegetables, garlic granules and ground ginger. Stir well and cook for 3–4 minutes until heated through.

5

Return the prawns to the pan and drizzle over soy sauce to taste. Stir everything together and cook for a further minute.

6

Season with black pepper and serve hot.

Tips & Notes

  • drain well and leave to cool slightly.
