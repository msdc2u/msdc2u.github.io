Pork Steak with Creamy Pepper Sauce

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serves 4
Low cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 4pork steaks
  • 200 mldouble cream
  • 1chicken stock cube
  • 1chicken stock cube
  • 1/2 tspSeasoning (Dried Only)
  • 1teaspoon garlic granules
  • 1teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tspStore Cupboard / Assumed
  • 1 tbspCooking oil
  • 1/2 tspSalt
  • 200 mlWater

Method

1

Season the pork steaks on both sides with salt, ground black pepper and garlic granules.

2

Heat a little oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the pork steaks and cook for 4–5 minutes on each side until cooked through and lightly golden. Remove from the pan and set aside.

3

Lower the heat slightly. Dissolve the stock cube in 200 ml hot water and pour into the pan, stirring to scrape up any bits from the bottom.

4

Pour in the double cream and add the ground black pepper. Simmer gently for 5–7 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

5

Return the pork steaks to the pan and spoon the sauce over them. Heat for 2–3 minutes.

6

Serve hot with potatoes, rice or vegetables.
