Mediterranean Halloumi, Pepper & Potato Bake

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 45 mins
Serves 4
Mid cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 4 servingsMain Ingredients
  • 400 ghalloumi (2 × 200 g packs)
  • 2 large potatoes (sliced)
  • 2peppers (sliced)
  • 2 tbspStore Cupboard / Assumed
  • 2 tbspcooking oil
  • 1 tspDried oregano
  • 1 tspGarlic granules
  • 1/2 tspSalt
  • 1/2 tspBlack pepper

Method

1

Preheat the oven to 200°C (180°C fan).

2

Add the sliced potatoes and peppers to a large bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle over the dried oregano, garlic granules, salt and black pepper. Toss well to coat.

3

Spread the vegetables out in a large baking dish or roasting tin and bake for 25 minutes, until the potatoes begin to soften and turn golden.

4

Remove from the oven and arrange the sliced halloumi on top of the vegetables.

5

Return to the oven and bake for a further 15–20 minutes, until the halloumi is golden and lightly crisp and the potatoes are tender.

6

Remove from the oven and leave to rest for 5 minutes before serving.
