Cheesy Gnocchi Bake with Spinach and Tomato

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serves 4
Low cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 2x 500g gnocchi (fresh or shelf-stable)
  • 2tins chopped tomatoes (400g each)
  • 100 gfresh spinach
  • 4 garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 125 gmozzarella (torn)
  • 75 gcheddar (grated)
  • 1 tspdried Italian herbs
  • 1 tspsugar
  • 1 tbspcooking oil
  • to tastesalt and pepper (to taste)

Method

1

Heat the oven to 200C.

2

Heat the olive oil in an ovenproof frying pan or casserole dish over a medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.

3

Add the chopped tomatoes, dried herbs and sugar. Season well. Simmer for 5 minutes.

4

Stir in the spinach until wilted.

5

Add the gnocchi straight into the sauce and stir to coat.

6

Scatter the mozzarella and cheddar over the top.

7

Bake in the oven for 20-22 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbling and the gnocchi is cooked through.

8

Leave to cool slightly before serving.
