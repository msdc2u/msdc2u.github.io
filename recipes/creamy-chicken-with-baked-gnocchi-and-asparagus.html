Creamy Chicken with Baked Gnocchi & Asparagus

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 40 mins
Serves 4
Low cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 600 gChicken thighs – 600g (diced)
  • 500 gGnocchi – 500g
  • 1 bunchAsparagus – 1 bunch (trimmed and cut into pieces)
  • 1 mediumOnion – 1 medium (finely chopped)
  • 1 tspGarlic granules – 1 tsp
  • 300 mlChicken stock – 300ml
  • 200 mlDouble cream – 200ml
  • 100 gGrated cheddar – 100g
  • 1 tbspCooking oil – 1 tbsp
  • to tasteSalt & black pepper – to taste

Method

1

Cook the chicken

2

Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium heat and cook the diced chicken thighs until lightly golden.

3

Soften the onion

4

Add the chopped onion and cook for a few minutes until softened.

5

Add seasoning

6

Stir in the garlic granules, salt and pepper.

7

Make the sauce

8

Pour in the chicken stock and double cream, stirring until combined.

9

Add gnocchi and asparagus

10

Stir the gnocchi and asparagus into the sauce and cook for 2–3 minutes.

11

Transfer to oven dish

12

Pour everything into a baking dish and scatter the grated cheddar over the top.

13

Bake

14

Bake at 200°C for 20 minutes until bubbling and lightly golden on top.

15

Serve

16

Leave for a few minutes before serving so the sauce thickens slightly.
