Crispy Chicken Katsu with Rice

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serves 4
Low cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 600 gchicken thighs (boneless and skinless)
  • 80 gpanko breadcrumbs
  • 50 gplain flour
  • 2medium eggs (beaten)
  • 2 tbspcooking oil
  • 1medium onion (diced)
  • 2medium carrots (peeled and roughly chopped)
  • 1 tspgarlic granules
  • 2 tbspmild curry powder
  • 1 tspturmeric
  • 400 mlcoconut milk
  • 200 mlchicken stock
  • 300 gbasmati rice (dry weight)
  • salt and pepper (to season)

Method

1

Make the sauce first. Heat 1 tbsp cooking oil in a saucepan. Soften the onion and carrot for 8–10 minutes. Add garlic granules, curry powder and turmeric and cook for 1 minute.

2

Pour in the coconut milk and chicken stock. Simmer for 12 minutes until slightly thickened. Blitz smooth with a stick blender. Season and set aside.

3

Flatten each chicken thigh between two sheets of cling film to an even thickness. Season well.

4

Set up three bowls — flour, beaten egg, panko breadcrumbs. Coat each thigh in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs.

5

Heat 1 tbsp cooking oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Fry the chicken for 5–6 minutes each side until golden and cooked through. Alternatively, oven bake at 200°C for 28–30 minutes.

6

Cook the rice according to packet instructions. Slice the chicken, pour over the katsu sauce and serve on top of the rice.

Tips & Notes

  • boneless and skinless
