Cheesy Sausage Quesadillas

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Serves 4
Low cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 4Ingredients
  • 8Pork sausages
  • 4Tortilla wraps
  • 200 gGrated cheese
  • 1 medium onionOnion or peppers (optional)
  • 1 tbspCooking oil

Method

1

Cook the sausages in a pan until browned and cooked through.

2

Slice the sausages into small pieces.

3

Lay a tortilla wrap flat and scatter over sausage pieces and grated cheese.

4

Add onion or peppers if using.

5

Fold the wrap in half.

6

Toast in a dry pan or air fryer until golden and the cheese has melted.

7

Cut into wedges and serve.
