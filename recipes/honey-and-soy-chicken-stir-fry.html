Honey & Soy Chicken Stir Fry

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves 4
Low cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 700 gBoneless skinless chicken thighs – 700g (cut into chunks or strips)
  • 300 gEgg noodles – 300g (dry weight)
  • 2Peppers – 2 mixed (sliced)
  • 1 largeOnion – 1 large (sliced)
  • 150 gFrozen peas – 150g
  • 1 tspGar garlic granules – 1 tsp
  • 3 tbspSoy sauce – 3 tbsp
  • 1 tbspHoney – 1 tbsp
  • 2 tbspCooking oil – 2 tbsp
  • to tasteSalt & black pepper

Method

1

Cook the noodles according to packet instructions, then drain and set aside.

2

Heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok over medium-high heat.

3

Add the sliced chicken and cook until lightly browned and cooked through.

4

Add the onion and peppers and cook for a few minutes until just softened.

5

Stir in the garlic granules.

6

In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce and honey.

7

Pour the sauce into the pan and stir to coat the chicken and vegetables.

8

Add the peas and cooked noodles, tossing everything together until heated through.

9

Season to taste and serve.

Tips & Notes

  • cut into chunks or strips
