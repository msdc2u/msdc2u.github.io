Pork Steak Chunk Pasta

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Serves 4
Low cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 4 servingsIngredients
  • 600 gPork steaks (cut into chunks)
  • 320 gPasta
  • 1onion (chopped)
  • 1 tspGarlic granules
  • 1 tspPaprika
  • 1× 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbspTomato purée
  • 1 tbspCooking oil
  • to tasteSalt & black pepper

Method

1

Cook the pasta according to packet instructions, then drain.

2

Heat a little oil in a large pan and brown the pork chunks.

3

Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.

4

Stir in the garlic granules and paprika.

5

Add the chopped tomatoes and a small spoon of tomato purée.

6

Simmer for 10–15 minutes until the pork is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.

7

Stir through the cooked pasta and serve.
