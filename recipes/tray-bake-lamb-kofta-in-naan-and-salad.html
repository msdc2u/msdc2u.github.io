Tray Bake Lamb Kofta in Naan & Salad

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 22 mins
Serves 4
Mid cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • For the koftas:
  • 500 gminced lamb
  • 1onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tspGarlic granules
  • 1 tspGround cumin
  • 1 tspGround coriander
  • 1 tspPaprika
  • 1/2 tsp saltSalt & pepper
  • 4naan breads
  • 1 headLettuce (cucumber, tomato)
  • 1 tbspCooking oil

Method

1

Heat oven to 200°C.

2

Mix lamb, onion, spices and seasoning.

3

Shape into kofta logs.

4

Bake for 18–22 minutes until cooked.

5

Warm naan and serve with salad.
