Mexican Chicken & Chorizo Rice Bowl

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves 4
Mid cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • 4Main Ingredients
  • 4chicken thighs (diced)
  • 200 gchorizo (diced)
  • 150 gcrème fraîche
  • 1medium onion (diced)
  • 600 gcooked rice
  • 1Store Cupboard / Assumed
  • 1 tbspCooking oil
  • 1 tspCajun seasoning
  • 1/2 tspSalt
  • 1/4 tspBlack pepper

Method

1

Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.

2

Add the diced chicken and onion. Cook for 3–4 minutes until the onion softens and the chicken starts to brown.

3

Add the diced chorizo and cook for another 5 minutes, letting it release its oils and crisp slightly.

4

Sprinkle in the Cajun seasoning and stir for 1 minute to coat everything evenly.

5

Add the crème fraîche and cooked rice. Stir gently until everything is well mixed and heated through.

6

Taste and season with salt and pepper as needed, remembering the chorizo is already salty.

7

Serve hot.
