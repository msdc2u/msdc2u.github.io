Sticky Pork with Noodles

Prep: 0 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Serves 4
Low cost

Ingredients

Serves 4 people
  • Ingredients
  • 500 gpork mince
  • 300 gdried egg noodles
  • 1medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbspsoy sauce
  • 2 tbspsweet chilli sauce
  • 1 tbsptomato ketchup
  • 1 tspgarlic granules
  • 1 tspground ginger
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Store cupboard (assumed:)
  • Cooking oil

Method

1

Cook the noodles

2

Bring a large pan of water to the boil. Cook the noodles according to packet instructions. Drain and set aside.

3

Cook the pork and onion

4

Heat a little oil in a large pan or wok over a medium heat. Add the pork mince and chopped onion and cook for 6–8 minutes, stirring, until the pork is browned and the onion has softened.

5

Add flavour

6

Stir in the garlic granules, ground ginger and black pepper. Cook for 1 minute.

7

Make it sticky

8

Add the soy sauce, sweet chilli sauce and ketchup. Stir well and simmer for 3–4 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the pork.

9

Combine

10

Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss until fully coated in the sticky sauce.

11

Serve

12

Serve hot straight from the pan.
