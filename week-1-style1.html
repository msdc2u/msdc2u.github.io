Picnic Tea

Not every evening needs a full cooked meal. Picnic Tea is our relaxed, low-effort option — a chance to use up whatever bits and pieces are left from the week’s shopping without needing to cook or plan anything complicated. It’s especially handy on kid club nights when you need something quick, or when you’re eating on the go. So get that picnic blanket out — even if it’s only on the living room floor.
